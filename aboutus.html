Our Purpose

Hey there, fellow food lover! We’re four international students who know exactly what it’s like to crave the comforting, soul-warming tastes of home while living far away. That’s why we created this space—to gather, simplify, and share the authentic, traditional recipes from our homelands (and beyond!) so you can recreate those "just like mom/grandma made it" flavors in your own kitchen. From quick weeknight fixes to festive family dishes, each recipe comes with a dash of cultural love, easy-to-follow steps, and a sprinkle of fun. Because food isn’t just about eating, it’s about connection, memories, and joy.
